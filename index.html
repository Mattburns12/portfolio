About

Hi, my name is Matthew Burns and I am a rising senior at Independence High School in the Academy of Engineering. I am Revit certified user and I won first place a state competiotion in 2D architecture where we had to use Revit to model a townhouse that met certain requirements.

I am also apart of the Tech Charlotte internship program through MYEP where we earn a variety of skills in the technical field that we can use in th internship we are placed in or in the careers that we choose. I have learned the basics of a variety of coding languages, how to do market research, and construct different levels of prototypes.
